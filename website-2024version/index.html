Oct 19, 2023

The time-convolutionless master equation is a powerful technique to model the non-Markovian dynamics of open quantum systems. In this arXiv, we introduce a diagrammatic representation of the time-local generator to arbitrary orders and also a non-perturbative method to approximate it by a nested time-ordered exponential.
